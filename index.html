How we
work

Our policies are somewhat lax in several aspects, and while we always maintain some leeway for each project, we try to offer a fair and safe space for each developer.

1

Propose a Project

It can be a landing page, a database, a videogame mod or something like that.

2

Define certain policies

Things like "for-profit or non-profit projects", number of members, category or the minimum level of prior knowledge required.

3

Member Search

Through Discord, we help you to find members interested in participating according to the requirements of your project.

4

A suitable space

In our server, we assign you a category and specific channels according to your needs.

5

Help and Advice

If requested, we can support you with some suggestions/advice in any area, be it administration, task distribution or more technical parts of the code.
